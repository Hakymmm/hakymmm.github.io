03

Visit

Purpose

To conduct a comprehensive on-site evaluation of A'Famosa Water World's tourism competitiveness through direct observation, data collection, and visitor interaction. This visit aims to assess the attraction's performance across key competitiveness indicators including facilities, services, accessibility, and overall visitor experience.

Date, Time & Duration

Date: 15th September 2025
Time: 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM
Duration: 9 hours

Transportation

Mode: Shuttle (Car)
Distance: 30 km
Travel Time: 40 minutes

12:30

Overall Experience

Our site visit to A'Famosa water World provided valuable insights into the operational aspects, visitor experience, and overall competitiveness of the attraction. We observed various facilities, services, and management practices that contribute to the park's appeal and areas for potential improvement.

The visit enabled us to gather first-hand data and observations that form the basis of our competitiveness analysis.
