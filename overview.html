Attraction Overview

Location

A'Famosa water World is located in the historic city of Melaka, Malaysia. Situated in a prime location accessible from major highways, the waterpark offers easy access for both local and international visitors. The strategic location combines the charm of historical Melaka with modern entertainment facilities.

Visit Official Website

History

Established as one of Melaka's premier family entertainment destinations, A'Famosa water World has been serving visitors for several years. The park was developed to complement Melaka's growing tourism industry, providing a modern recreational facility that appeals to families and adventure seekers visiting the UNESCO World Heritage city.
